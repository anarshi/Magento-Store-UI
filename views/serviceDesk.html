Payment

We currently accept payment by Visa, MasterCard, iDeal, Bitcoins and PayPal. If you don’t use any of these options, please contact our customer service here.

Upon receiving your order, we carry out a standard pre-authorisation on your payment method to ensure there are sufficient funds to fulfill the transaction. We will only charge the pre-authorisation when the order is packed and ready for shipment. If the billing information is incorrect or incomplete, we may contact you for confirmation before your order is shipped.

Order confirmation

You will receive an order confirmation email a few minutes after you place your order. Please check your junk or spam folders if it does not appear in your inbox.

Please review the order confirmation and contact us here if you see any errors. You can also review the details of your order from your account, under Order History.

Order status

To check your order status:

1) Log in to your account;
2) Under Recent Orders, select your order;
3) From here, you can view order details and track your order(s).

You’ll also receive an e-mail with a link leading to the website of the forwarder to track your order. If you haven’t received this mail after 1 day, please check your junk or spam filter.

Order changes and cancellations

If you would like to change/cancel your order, please contact our customer service as soon as possible. If your order hasn’t been dispatched, we are in most cases able to help you. Please note that there’s not much to do when your order has been sent.

Seasonal sales, new arrivals and promotions

Signing up for our newsletter is the best way to receive updates about new arrivals, upcoming sales and promotions. You can subscribe when creating an account.
