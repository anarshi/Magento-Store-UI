The start

ETQ, founded in 2010, was born under the mindset of eliminating over accessorized branding and focusing primary on letting the quality of the product speak for itself. All our designs are generated under three standard rules: elegant, timeless, and quality. Slowly, this belief grew into a range of footwear and accessories that live up to the standard ETQ stands for.

The storm

With a strong focus and conceptual thinking, ETQ is driven to create aesthetic yet functional, enduring footwear that distinctively set apart from the norms. Prioritizing customer satisfaction and creating aspiring elegant quality above everything, we have successfully garnered a substantial amount of acknowledgement among them.

Getting serious

Smart, but yet simple designs often require a lot of energy to find and money to buy. With our vision in mind, instead of blindly searching, we decided to just grab a pen and a paper, and started designing from scratch. What we aim for is just a line of our favorite pieces that we can live in on a daily. The result is a line of footwear and accessories that are seasonless with reasonable pricing. Exactly what was lacking in the market before.

The webstore

Not only does ETQ aim to provide functionality in its footwear and accessories, but also aim to implement an effortless and thoughtful online service for our customers.

With the sole mindset of creating a new digital experience with smooth and easily understandable animation, ETQ makes online shopping so much faster with no thought needed.
